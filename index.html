About GAMANTRA-23

Research and Innovation in Science and Technology (RISE). This year in 2023 we have branded this as GAMANTRA-23, meaning GAMAN+YANTRA. It symbolizes the enlivenment of the machine based on research and innovation. Various institutions, research scholars, industries, students and faculty, funding agencies, bankers and every individual interested in the future of science and technology are invited(society).

OUR AIMS

Gamantra's objective is to propagate state-of-the-art research and encourage research culture among institutions. To get social recognition due to the inventors as the creators of products for the consumer and / or industry. To stimulate public interest in the invention process through public relations, publicity, and other promotions. To encourage young minds to ponder upon current issues and seek innovative and feasible solutions..
